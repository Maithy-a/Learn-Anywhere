# KISWAHILI NOTES (Grade 4-6)

## Habari za asubuhi! = Good morning!
## Habari za mchana! = Good afternoon!
## Habari za jioni! = Good evening!
## Kwa heri! = Goodbye!
## Asante! = Thank you!
## Samahani = Sorry

## Mamilioni ya watu wanasema Kiswahili nchini Kenya, Tanzania, na Uganda.

## Sentences:
- Mimi ni mwanafunzi. = I am a student.
- Ninafuraha kusoma. = I am happy to read.
- Unakwenda wapi? = Where are you going?

## Mofimu:
- Mtu → Watu (person → people)
- Kitu → Vitu (thing → things)
- Mahali → Mahali (place → places)

## Numbers:
- Moja, Mbili, Tatu, Nne, Tano, Sita, Saba, Nane, Tisa, Kumi
