# GRADE 1: ENVIRONMENTAL ACTIVITIES

## Living Things
- Grow, eat, breathe, move
- Examples: People, animals, plants

## Non-Living Things
- Do not grow or eat
- Examples: Rock, water, chair

## Parts of a Plant
- Roots: Hold plant in soil
- Stem: Carries water
- Leaves: Make food
- Flower: Makes seeds

## Animals
- Mammals: Feed milk (cow, goat)
- Birds: Have feathers and fly
- Insects: 6 legs (ant, bee)

## Weather
- Sunny: Bright sky
- Rainy: Water falls from clouds
- Windy: Air moves fast
